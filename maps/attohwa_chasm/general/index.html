Notes

This area is divided into two parts, east side and west side. The west side contains tougher monsters, and leads to Boneyard Gully. The east side have easier monsters and a high hill that can be climbed.

Paths on either sides are often blocked off by black gas, some of them will clear from time to time, others are always there.

To climb the hill on the east side, start from the spot marked "Starting Point". Look for very narrow and steep paths up the hill, and keep climbing counter clock-wise until near the top. Sometimes the path will be blocked off by more black smoke. Some will clear, some will always be there so you need to drop to a lower level. It's quite difficult to climb to the top, and may take many many tries !

Luminant: When you select this object, you will be given a key item called "Mimeo Stone". This key item will slowly fade away.
If you are able to make it to the top of Parradamo Tor and take this object to the Cradle of Rebirth, you will be rewarded with an item.
