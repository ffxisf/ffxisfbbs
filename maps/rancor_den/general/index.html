Notes

In this area, to get past doors with lanterns in front you must first kill certain types of tonberries that drops an "Unlit lantern".

To activate the doors, people with unlit lanterns must first light their lantern at the Altar of Rancor of matching colour, then light the lanterns before the doors.

If there are less people than the lanterns labeled before the doors, those with Unlit lanterns will need to run back to the Altar of Rancor to complete the rest.
