Notes

This place has two types of devices throughout the area. One is "The Mute", the other is "The Afflictor". Touching The Mute will give you the status Silence, walking by an Afflictor without the silence status will give you the Curse status. So to get by an area with both devices you must have either the status silence or curse.

It is recommended that you silence yourself first before walking passed the curse devices, and make sure mages bring echo drops.
